What is ashcon?

Hashcon is initiated this year by the Cyber Security Club, serves as a pivotal platform for students, research scholars, and companies to converge and exchange valuable insights on the rapidly evolving landscape of cybersecurity. This conference aims to foster connections, share knowledge, and provide exposure to cutting-edge advancements in the field. The Cyber Security Club of IIIT Kottayam leads the revolution of this field among the students with its own Cyber Security Department honed and equipped with the best tools it has to offer.

Why Join us?

  • Expert Talks

    The seminar will provide participants with insights from industry experts and experienced professionals as well as a variety of perspectives and expertise in the area of cybersecurity.

  • Training Sessions

    Facilitate hands-on training sessions to improve attendance' practical abilities and provide students with the newest cybersecurity technologies and methods.

  • Attack Stalls

    Students set up attack stalls to simulate realistic cyber-attack scenarios to offer the participants an opportunity to practice understanding and countering threats in real life.

  • Cyber Exhibition

    This student-curated exhibition provides participants with the chance to showcase their innovative work and projects.A dynamic platform for students to interact with industry leaders, this program fosters valuable connections and recognizes their contributions in cybersecurity.

  • Hackathon

    Problem Solving: Encouraging participants to collaboratively solve cybersecurity challenges within a competitive environment. Fostering Innovation: Promoting creative solutions to real-world cybersecurity problems.

  • Capture The Flag (CTF)

    CTF is the contest to demonstrate your expertise in cyber security.

  • Stalls for Companies

    Providing companies with a platform to showcase products, services, and career opportunities. Connecting companies with potential talents among the student and research scholar participants.
